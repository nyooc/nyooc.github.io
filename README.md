## Hike: Appalachian Trail Part I (2013)

I was a NOBO (north-bound) BASH (big-ass section hiker) on the Appalachian Trail in the Fall 
of 2013. It took 74 days to get from Springer Mountain, Georgia to the Appalachian Trail train stop
at the end of New York, about 2/3 of the length of the trail. The posts are in Hungarian.
